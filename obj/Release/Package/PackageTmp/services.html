What We Offer

Discover the powerful features that our software offers specifically for breeders:

Central Dashboard

One-click design keeps everything you need in the palm of your hand!

Appointments & Reminders

Manage calendars for animal and for all activities.

Contact Information

For each animal, store and access contact info for owners, veterinarian, farrier, insurance, association and more.

Detailed Notes

Record notes on almost every type of record that can be recorded in Ranch Manager. From Animals, to Locations, to Owners and Contacts.

Event Management

Any event that can happen to an animal you can track. Specific events supported include: animal sale, animal treatment (vaccinations, etc), artificial insemination/breeding, birth, and death. A custom event is available for you to enter any custom event information. Events automatically show up in the Rancher’s Calendar so you can easily keep a history of your operation. Enter a single event that affects multiple animals with an intuitive and easy-to-use interface.

Feed Management

Apply feeding plans to your locations and track the amount of food consumed by each animal. Animal Owner will calculate the cost and amount of feed consumed by animals in the location where feed is applied, saving you a lot of math and providing valuable information.
